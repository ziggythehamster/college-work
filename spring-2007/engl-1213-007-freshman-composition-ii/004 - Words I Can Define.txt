WORDS I CAN DEFINE
==================

quality - what I think makes something good and what makes it bad
catastrophuck - a catastrophe that ends up messing things up for everyone; a description of my week
tranquility - what puts me into the "zone" of wanting to do things
industry - a word I'm tired of hearing when speaking of the IT industry; purpose is to convince people to stop using this word unless they've been in said industry recently
cacophony - a terrible sound; a description of music that sucks
